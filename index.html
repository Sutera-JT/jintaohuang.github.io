About Me

I am a fourth-year undergraduate student in the HUST CSE Department. My research interest is in data-based measurement and program analysis for security. Currently, I focus on blockchain.

I mainly work in Security PRIDE research group during my undergraduate study under Prof. Haoyu Wang at HUST. Also, I have the fortune to work with Prof. Gareth Tyson at HKUST and Prof. Konstantin Beznosov at UBC.

I'm always open to collaborations. If you have any interesting ideas, please feel free to get in touch with me!
